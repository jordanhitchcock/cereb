    About CEREB

    Team

    CEREB was formed by real estate executives and home service professionals that are passionate about solving the need for a frictionless, one-stop home automation solution. CEREB is backed by a principal in a leading VC specializing in the future of mobility and living spaces.

    CEREB's leadership brings together industry experts from firms that have managed 100,000+ living units, led development in personal transportation and built successful, large-scale home service businesses.

    Engineering & Privacy

    CEREB's chief engineers hail from the aviation and financial services industries, where reliability and security are paramount.

    CEREB's team has over 15 years of experience in building innovative software products for secure and easy-to-use communications systems.
